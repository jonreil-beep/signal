# PRODUCT.md — Signal

## One-line pitch
AI-powered career positioning and job search copilot for experienced professionals with complex backgrounds — so you apply smarter, not more.

---

## The problem

Experienced professionals with wide-ranging backgrounds (strategy, creative, brand, operations, consulting) often struggle not because they lack skills — but because their profile doesn't immediately answer the question hiring teams ask first:

**"What role should we hire this person for?"**

The result: strong candidates apply broadly, get little traction, and can't tell why. Generic job boards make this worse. They surface volume, not fit. They don't tell you how to position yourself, which openings are worth your time, or how to tailor your approach per role.

---

## The solution

A job search copilot that does five things well:

1. **Diagnoses your positioning** — maps your strongest role clusters, surfaces risks a recruiter will notice, and tells you how your background is likely to be read
2. **Scores job postings** against your profile with clear reasoning and a recruiter concern flag
3. **Generates a tailoring brief** before each application — what to emphasize, what language to mirror, what concern to preempt
4. **Produces application materials** — cover letter, outreach messages, resume bullets, interview prep — all calibrated to your actual background and the specific role
5. **Keeps your search organized** — every scored job saved with fit score, prep guide, and pipeline status

---

## Target user (v1)

**Experienced professionals making a deliberate move** — especially people whose backgrounds are strong but not easy to translate into a clean, obvious next step:

- Strong track record but struggles to frame it for specific roles
- Making a pivot, stepping up, or navigating overqualification concerns
- Wants to stop wasting time on roles that were never a real match
- Tired of generic advice that misses the nuance of their background

**Primary test user:** Holly — senior research/strategy professional with a broad, high-quality background spanning policy evaluation, strategy consulting, and market research.

**Future users (v2+):** Directors, VPs, heads of function in creative, marketing, product, strategy, and operations. Also career coaches serving this population.

---

## What success looks like (v1)

- User uploads resume and gets a clear map of best-fit role categories with honest positioning risks
- Pastes a job description and gets a fit score + recruiter concern flag in under 20 seconds
- Before applying, gets a tailoring brief that tells them exactly what to emphasize
- Cover letter, outreach, and resume bullets feel like their voice — not AI-generated
- Applies to fewer jobs, better targeted, with sharper positioning
- Holly says yes, she'd pay for it

---

## What this is NOT (v1)

- Not a job board or aggregator
- Not an automated applicant system
- Not a LinkedIn optimizer (v2)
- Not a multi-user platform (v2)

---

## Design principles

- **Honesty over encouragement.** Tell the user where they look weak, not just where they look strong.
- **Reasoning over scores.** Every score has an explanation. No black boxes.
- **Second person for analysis, first person for documents.** Analysis outputs (fit scores, clusters, risks) address the user directly as "you." Sendable documents (cover letters, outreach, follow-ups) are written in first person "I/my."
- **Speed.** Each core feature should return results in one interaction.
- **Output-first UI.** Show analysis results by default. Hide inputs unless the user is editing.
- **Signal gives you the raw material. You bring the judgment.**

---

## Monetization thinking (early stage)

**Primary offer:** 90-day career positioning and search sprint
- Profile analysis, role clustering, fit scoring, recruiter concern flags, prep guides, resume rewrites, LinkedIn headline, interview prep
- Pricing: $79–149 for 90 days

**Secondary offer (v2):** Coach seat — per-seat platform for career coaches serving this population

**Why bootstrapping first:**
- Product needs tight feedback loops with real users
- Real moat is trust and output quality, not scale
- 500 delighted paying users matter more than 50,000 casual free users
- Especially true for directors, VPs, and execs who value nuance
