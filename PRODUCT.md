# PRODUCT.md — Job Search Copilot

## One-line pitch
An AI-powered job search copilot that turns a broad, senior background into a focused strategy — so you apply smarter, not more.

---

## The problem

Experienced professionals with wide-ranging backgrounds (strategy, research, consulting, policy) often struggle not because they lack skills — but because their profile doesn't immediately answer the question hiring teams ask first:

**"What role should we hire this person for?"**

The result: strong candidates apply broadly, get little traction, and can't tell why.

Generic job boards make this worse. They surface volume, not fit. They don't tell you how to position yourself, which openings are worth your time, or how to tailor your approach per role.

---

## The solution

A job search copilot that does three things well:

1. **Identifies your strongest role clusters** from your background
2. **Scores job postings** against your profile with clear reasoning
3. **Generates a tailoring brief** before each application so you apply with intention

---

## Target user (v1)

**Holly.** Senior research/strategy professional with a broad, high-quality background spanning policy evaluation, strategy consulting, and market research. She has the skills — the app helps her aim them.

**Future users (v2+):** Experienced professionals with wide analytical/strategy profiles — consultants, operators, researchers, policy-to-industry pivots — who are getting silence, not interviews.

---

## What success looks like (v1)

- Holly uploads her resume and gets a clear map of her best-fit role categories
- She pastes a job description and gets a fit score + honest reasoning in under 10 seconds
- Before applying, she gets a tailoring brief that tells her exactly what to emphasize
- She no longer wastes time on low-probability applications
- She applies to fewer jobs, better targeted, with sharper positioning

---

## What this is NOT (v1)

- Not a job board or aggregator
- Not an automated applicant system
- Not a resume rewriter (v2)
- Not a LinkedIn optimizer (v2)

---

## Design principles

- **Honesty over encouragement.** Tell the user where they look weak, not just where they look strong.
- **Reasoning over scores.** Every score has an explanation. No black boxes.
- **Speed.** Each core feature should return results in one interaction.
- **Scrappy first.** This is an internal tool. Ship it working, not pretty.
